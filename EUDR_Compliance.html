The Challenge

EUDR Demands Deep Supply Chain Visibility

The EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) requires businesses placing specified commodities on the EU market to demonstrate their products are deforestation-free and legally produced.

With plot-level geolocation requirements, mandatory due diligence obligations, and significant penalties for non-compliance, the regulation demands robust systems and genuine supply chain transparency ‚Äî not just paper commitments.

For many businesses, the challenge is not willingness to comply ‚Äî it's the practical complexity of achieving it across multi-tier global supply chains with legacy supplier relationships.
